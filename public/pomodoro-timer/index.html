Frequently asked questions

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method: 25 minutes of focused work, followed by a 5-minute break. After four sessions, you take a longer break of 15–30 minutes. In Calcup, all durations are fully adjustable.

Is registration required to use the timer?

No. Calcup runs entirely in your browser with no account needed. All tasks, timer settings, and session history are stored locally on your device.

Can I see my current task next to the timer?

Yes. Calcup's task planner is integrated with the Pomodoro timer. When you activate a task, it appears directly above the timer display so you stay focused on the right work.

Can I adjust the session length?

Yes. Use the settings toggle inside the timer to change the duration of focus blocks, short breaks, and long breaks independently.
